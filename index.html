PROVIDING HEALTHY FOOD FOR THE ELDERLY

(Our mission is to limit the hassle of having to worry about our elder family members being able to eat good wholesome meals when nobody's around to assist them,or when medication restrictions are a factor along with time being so crucial with the hustle and bustle of everyday life.)

Who Am I Helping,What Do I Want To Help Them With

Im helping the elderly who have problems with mobibilty when it comes to leaving their place of residence. Iwant to be able to help them with healthy food preparation.

How Do I Want To Help Them

By ensuring that their meals are prepared as healthy and delicious as possible according to medication restrictions if any.

Things That Cause Unhealthy Diets For The Elderly

  • Nobody with enough time to devote to them to make sure they're eating right and following dietary restrictions from medications or sickness.
  • Poor time management with family members
  • Not following proper diets where medication or exercise is involved.
  • When they have to fend for themselves to eat when nobody's around.
  • ALL different types of medical issues such as heart attacks,strokes,allergic reactions from medications;not to mention severe sickness or possible death.
